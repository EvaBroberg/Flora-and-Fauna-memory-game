PLAYING FLORA AND FAUNA

Please enter the name of your choice and click the button to start the game. On the right you can see icons to toggle sound or come back to game rules if you'd like to refresh your memory. As you start the game you will see rows of cards displayed fave down, the timer will start and the number of turns will add up as you turn two cards. If cards have matched they will stay face up and if not they will flip back over.

When all cards are facing up you proceed to next level

You will have 60 seconds to complete each level without exceeding the maximum of 30 turns. There are 3 levels in total, you WIN the game when the 3rd level is completed!
